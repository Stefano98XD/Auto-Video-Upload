Auto-Video-Upload

Privacy Policy

Auto-Video-Upload is a personal content management application that allows users to upload and manage content on authorized social media accounts.

The application only accesses information that users explicitly authorize through TikTok's authentication process.

Authorized information may include basic profile information, access tokens, refresh tokens, and permissions required to upload and manage content.

User data is stored only as necessary to provide the application's functionality and maintain authorized access.

Auto-Video-Upload does not sell, rent, share, or distribute personal information to third parties.

Users may revoke the application's access at any time through their TikTok account settings.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information from unauthorized access.

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, users may contact the developer through the GitHub repository associated with this application.
